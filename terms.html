Terms & Privacy

A plain-language explanation, not a wall of legalese.
The short version: this is a small, self-hosted game hub. If you create an account, we store your email, a securely hashed password (or your Google account link, if you use that instead), whatever display name/bio/avatar you choose to add, and your high scores. We don't sell your data, run ads, or share it with anyone except Google itself when you choose to sign in with Google. There's no analytics or tracking beyond what's needed to keep you signed in.

1. What this site is

Game Hub is a small collection of browser games with an optional account system so your profile, avatar, and high scores can follow you across devices. It's hosted on GitHub Pages, and account data is stored using Google's Firebase (Authentication and Firestore) — there's no separate company or custom server behind it.

2. Creating an account

You can either:

  • Sign up with an email and password, or
  • Sign in with Google, which shares your name, email, and profile picture with this site so it can create a matching profile automatically.

Passwords are never stored or seen by this site at all — Firebase Authentication handles that entirely, and only tells this site whether the sign-in succeeded.

3. What we store

  • Account: your email address (used only for signing in — Firebase Authentication manages this, not this site directly)
  • Profile: display name, bio, and avatar image, if you choose to set them
  • Scores: your saved high score per game
  • Google accounts: the name, email, and profile picture Google shares at sign-in, used only to create and recognize your profile

We don't store payment information, precise location, or anything beyond what's listed above.

4. What we don't do

  • No ads, ad tracking, or ad networks
  • No selling or sharing your data with third parties
  • No analytics beyond what GitHub Pages or Firebase themselves may log for hosting purposes (e.g. basic server logs)

5. Your choices

You can stop using the site at any time. If you'd like your account data removed, contact whoever operates this particular deployment of Game Hub — since this is self-hosted software, the site operator (not the original developer of this template) is responsible for handling that request.

6. Acceptable use

  • Don't impersonate someone else or use a misleading display name/avatar
  • Don't upload illegal, abusive, or harmful images as your avatar
  • Don't attempt to break, exploit, or overload the sign-in or scoring system

7. No warranty

This is a small hobby-style project provided as-is, with no uptime guarantee and no warranty of any kind. High scores and profiles are stored in good faith but not guaranteed to be preserved forever.

8. Changes

This page may be updated as the site changes. Continuing to use the site after an update means you accept the current version.

This page is a plain, good-faith summary — not legal advice, and not reviewed by a lawyer. If you're deploying this for a large audience or a commercial product, it's worth having an actual privacy policy and terms of service drafted for your situation.
